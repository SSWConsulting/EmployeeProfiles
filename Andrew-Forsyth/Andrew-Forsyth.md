Andrew offers a unique blend of extensive marketing experience with advanced video production skills and the ability to lead and manage teams.  

After starting his career with Canon Australia and completing an MBA at Maquarie Graduate School of Management, he started a new division at Canon Singapore marketing digital imaging devices across SE Asia. Then, after seven years with Microsoft including three as a senior marketing manager in the US subsidiary, Andrew started Pemberton Productions with the aim of produding high quality video & marketing communications that deliver business results.  

in 2019 Andrew joined forces with SSW and continues to provide production services with Pemberton Productions.
